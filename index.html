The Akan name generator!

The Akan people of Ghana have a culture of naming their children where the children are given names basing on day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born.This web application takes your birthday details and calculates the day of the week you were born and then depending on your gender, outputs your Akan Name.Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.This naming culture is mostly done in West Africa. Diverse ethnicities have variants and meanings as a symbol of cultural integrity and ancestral affiliation.

The Akan people use the Kwa language group's system of timekeeping, which is based on a six-day week ('nnanson'), with the seventh day inclusive. Children are given their first names as a 'day name'. This name corresponds to the day of the week in which they were born.
